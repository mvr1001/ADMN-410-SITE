About Us

Our History

Durham House of Pets was founded by Dean Anthony Rametta back in 1994. In the early days the company was a very small and only included two employees Dean and his wife Mary. The company was inspired by their pet Sydney who was a Black Lab. The company has grown massively over the past twenty years. Durham House of Pets used to only sell a select few items but now sells all sorts of pet supplies including Tanks, Food, and Toys.

Why Choose Us?

Durham House of Pets should be your number one choice for pet stores in the area. Our employees are very skilled at what we do and we as a team always make sure our customer are satisfied. We are a local pet store and are in the heart of Durham New Hampshire. We offer the most high quality products and high quality workers at a very convernient location.
